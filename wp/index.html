📱Step 1: Explore & Download Wallpapers

On the main Nothing Space interface, browse unique wallpapers featuring our stunning Depth Effect. Explore depth visuals, select the setups that define your aesthetic, and download them cleanly to your device.

🧩 Step 2: Open KLWP and Load the Theme

Open KLWP Live Wallpaper App → Tap on the folder icon (library) in the top-right corner, choose import and select the theme you want to apply (for example, NS.klwp, NS_2.klwp). Your chosen theme will now load in the KLWP App.

🎨 Step 3: Adjust the Theme (Optional)

You can customize or remove almost anything inside KLWP: wallpaper, dock, icons. Always hit the Save icon in the top-right corner after making changes.

🌍 Step 4: Set KLWP as Your Wallpaper

Tap the Save icon in KLWP. Then tap “Set Wallpaper” → “Home Screen & Lock Screen”. Go back to your home screen and enjoy your new live setup! 🎉

🧠 Bonus Tips

If KLWP theme is not working perfectly, open KLWP app → three dots (⋮) → App Settings → Advanced Options → Disable Parallel Rendering - Turn ON (default: off).

🚀 So go ahead — customize your Android!
